# Data Protection Policy for Customer Portal

## 1. Introduction
This document outlines the data protection measures implemented in the Customer Portal application in compliance with GDPR and other applicable data protection regulations.

## 2. Data Processing Activities

### 2.1 Types of Data Processed
- Personal Identifiable Information (PII)
  - Names
  - Email addresses
  - Phone numbers
  - Physical addresses
- Business Data
  - Account information
  - Transaction history
  - Communication records

### 2.2 Lawful Basis for Processing
- Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b))
- Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f))
- Consent where required

## 3. Data Protection Measures

### 3.1 Technical Measures
- End-to-end encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Database access logging and monitoring

### 3.2 Organizational Measures
- Data protection training for all staff
- Clear data handling procedures
- Regular access reviews
- Incident response procedures

## 4. Data Subject Rights

### 4.1 Right to Access
- Users can request copies of their personal data
- Response time: Within 30 days

### 4.2 Right to Rectification
- Users can request corrections to inaccurate data
- Updates processed within 15 days

### 4.3 Right to Erasure
- Users can request deletion of their data
- Subject to legal and business requirements

### 4.4 Data Portability
- Users can export their data in standard formats
- Available upon request

## 5. Data Retention
- Personal data retained for 7 years after last interaction
- Business data retained according to legal requirements
- Regular data cleanup procedures implemented

## 6. Third-Party Processors
- All third-party processors have signed Data Processing Agreements
- Regular audits of third-party security measures
- Immediate termination for security breaches

## 7. Breach Notification
- Data breaches reported to supervisory authority within 72 hours
- Affected individuals notified when high risk is identified
- Internal incident response team activated immediately
